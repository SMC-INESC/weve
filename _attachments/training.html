Training Examples

To help you to understand the types of music examples you will hear during the listening experiment and to practice using the interface to enter your ratings, there is a short experimental training phase to complete.

You can listen to the training music examples as many times as you wish by scrolling to the bottom of the page and clicking the Next Training Example button. Please note that in the main experiment you will hear each example only once. We recommend you use this opporunity to set the volume to a comfortable level. All of the music examples have approximately the same loudness.

We recommend that you take the experiment in a quiet environment, and that you use good quality loudspeakers or headphones to listen to the examples. Once you are comfortable with entering your ratings please click Start to begin the experiment.
